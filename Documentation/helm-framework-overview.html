The Big Picture

What the tool does

Your charts go in; clear trade ideas and honest results come out on screen - all on your own computer.

1. Watch

The tool follows your trading chart. Each time a price bar finishes, it takes a snapshot along with the key market details.

2. Ask the AI

An AI looks at that snapshot and suggests a possible trade - buy or sell, where to get in, where to set a safety exit, a profit target, and why.

3. Decide & Measure

A dashboard shows each suggestion, your real trades, your profit or loss, and what's happening in the markets that day. You always make the final call.

Getting a Trade Idea

Two ways to ask the AI

On demand

Press Ctrl+Shift+F on any chart, drag a box around what you want looked at, and the AI reads it. About one second once it's warmed up.

On a schedule (hands-off)

Let it check your charts on its own at a set pace. It steps aside during the market's quiet maintenance hours, ignores a frozen feed, and won't pile on new ideas while you're already in a trade.

What the AI actually sees - a live chart snapshot with your indicators and key price levels.

Choose Your AI

You pick the brain - and where it runs

The AI is the only part that can reach beyond your computer, and it's entirely your choice whether it does.

On your computer

A free AI model (Ollama) runs right on your PC. Fully private - nothing about your charts ever leaves the machine.

On your home network

Run that same AI on a spare computer with a good graphics card, and point The Helm at it. Still all within your own walls.

In the cloud

Prefer no setup and top-tier vision? Use Claude or OpenAI with a paid key. Convenient, but the chart is sent to that service.

You can even mix them - a private local model for reading charts, and a cloud model for digesting the day's economic news.

The Chart Watcher

The piece that watches your chart

What it sends to the AI

  • Every finished price bar, with a chart snapshot and market details
  • Live price movement, sent continuously as the market moves
  • A keyboard shortcut to grab the chart any time you want an instant read

The market details it includes

  • Trend strength and direction in the bigger picture
  • Important daily price levels and overnight highs and lows
  • Signals about shifts in the market's direction that traders watch

Good to know

  • After an update, the new version has to be copied into your trading platform and the platform restarted - otherwise it keeps running the old one.
  • It works safely even with several charts open at once, and won't double-count anything.
  • It only reports the same information you can see on your own chart, so you can always check what the AI is looking at.

Keeping the Numbers Honest

It never shows made-up results

How it stays honest

  • It matches each AI idea you actually traded to your real broker records.
  • If the two ever disagree, your real result wins - the displayed win or loss flips to match your account.
  • If it can't confidently match a trade, it says so plainly instead of guessing a number.
  • Every correction is written to a log you can open and read.

Why it matters

Your profit and loss come from what really happened in your account - not from a simulation or an estimate.

This runs quietly on its own every hour, and you can also trigger a fresh check any time.

Placing Trades & Safety

It only acts when you say so

The safeguards

  • You approve each idea individually - nothing is automatic.
  • It starts switched OFF and places practice (pretend) trades only by default.
  • Locked to one account you choose; it refuses to touch any other.
  • Only one trade per market at a time.
  • Auto-shutoff if your balance hits a floor you set, or you reach a daily loss limit.
  • An idea expires if it isn't filled within your time window.

The principle

The tool proposes; you decide. Always.

Nothing ever fires on its own. The helper only acts on trade ideas you've personally approved.
